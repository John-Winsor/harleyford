I have received the kind donation of a large quantity of historical data for which I record my gratitude.  This data covers much more than the property that today comprises Dainton Close, Harleyford, Bishops Green and the ex-coach house residence.  It includes the minutiae of the life and times of Henry Babbage and of his successors to the ownership of the property.

Regrettably, much of the material is beyond the scope of this website.  I have, nevertheless, included some information about Henry Prevost Babbage who purchased the land in 1872 and developed it by constructing the Dainton and Harleyford manor houses.  Also I have written a few words about Henry's illustrious father Charles Babbage who originated the concept of the programmable computer.
