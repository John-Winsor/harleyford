A map is a "snapshot" of a moment in history

Dainton manor house has been demolished and is replaced with the apartments marked "Dainton Close".

Harleyford manor house has followed the same fate and is replaced with the apartments marked "Harleyford".

On the north side of Upper Park Road, starting from the junction with Plaistow lane, there are two houses.
The first of these was demolished in recent times and was replaced with an apartment block.
The second is a fine old Victorian house, still standing (in 2015).
The construction of the apartments (in Upper Park Road) marked "Bishops Green" is now complete.
