My notes:

Initially the Harleyford estate, constructed by Park Rutland in 1967, was entirely privately owned.  In 1970 Bromley Council adopted the roadway (ORANGE) and the path (BLUE), and also the strip of land (GREEN) that separates them.  Hence the maintenance of these areas became the responsibility of the Council.  (The reason why the path was adopted was to legitimise the installation of Council street lighting).
