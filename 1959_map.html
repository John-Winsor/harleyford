A map is a "snapshot" of a moment in history

This map is not significantly different to that in 1909 or 1933.

Dainton and Harleyford manor houses are shown.

The ex-coach house is shown, adjacent to Dainton house.

A large building has been constructed in the vicinity of the Harleyford house.

Elsternwick House is shown near to the northern tip of the property.

On the north side of Upper Park Road, starting from the junction with Plaistow lane, is shown a row of five houses.
The first of these was demolished in recent times and was replaced with an apartment block.
The second is a fine old Victorian house, still standing (in 2015).
The adjacent three houses no longer exist, and mark the future location of Bishops Green.
The last of these became owned by the Church of England.  It was used as the vicarage for St. John's church in Park Road.
